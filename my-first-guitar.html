First Guitar Buying Guide

So you want to see about starting guitar lessons, but first you need a guitar. Here's a quick guide to help you out.

Here are some things to consider:

  • Do you want an electric or acoustic guitar?

    • Either one can be suitable for beginners. Electric guitars require more equipment, but are louder in performances.

  • Do you want a full-size, three-quarters or half-sized guitar?

    • Normally anyone younger than 6 y/o will want a half-sized guitar. A guitar can't be too small, but it can be too big.
